Elephant

Note taker with a classic interface.

Welcome to Elephant.

Elephant is a note taker with a classic interface you already know.

Keep your notes to yourself.

No accounts, no clouds. No feature bloat or redesigns, ever.

Features

  • Elephant stores notes in plain files and folders. No databases and no migration.

  • No accounts or clouds, your notes stay with you. For syncing use Dropbox, Github, rsync, ...

  • Cross-platform for Mac, Windows and Linux.

  • Built for longevity with a classic interface. New versions will never offer weird functionality. There will never be a chat feature and it will never show stuff from Financial Times.

  • Premium is free, no ads.

  • Open source (soon).
